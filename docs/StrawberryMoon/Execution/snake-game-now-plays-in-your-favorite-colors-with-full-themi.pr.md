# Press Release

## Headline
Snake Game Now Plays in Your Favorite Colors with Full Theming and Local High Score Storage

## Summary
Snake Game introduces comprehensive theming support and enhanced score persistence, allowing players to customize the entire visual experience while permanently tracking their achievement history. From cyberpunk teal to retro greens, players can now personalize every element and watch their best scores persist reliably across browser sessions, devices, and updates. The theming system launches with five pre-built themes, full custom color support, and robust local storage integration for infinite personalization and score tracking.

## Problem Statement
Players love the instant accessibility and minimalist design of Snake Game, but a one-size-fits-all color scheme limits personal expression and doesn't accommodate different aesthetic preferences or accessibility needs. Additionally, high scores sometimes vanish due to browser data clearing or confusion about where scores are stored, frustrating players invested in tracking their progress. Office workers, developers, and casual gamers want visual customization and reliable score persistence without sacrificing the clean, distraction-free experience that makes the game perfect for work breaks.

## Solution
The theming system lets players choose from curated color palettes or build completely custom themes, controlling snake gradient colors, food particle glow, grid overlay, background, text, and accent colors. All themes are stored in browser local storage, persisting across sessions and devices. Simultaneously, the enhanced high score storage system automatically saves both current session scores and all-time best scores to local storage with transparent data persistence indicators, so players always know their achievements are safely preserved. The lightweight theme engine maintains the single-file architecture while delivering unlimited visual customization and reliable score tracking without compromising performance or gameplay speed.

## Key Features
- Five pre-built themes including Cyberpunk, Retro Green, Neon Pink, Ocean Blue, and Monochrome
- Full custom color picker for every visual element in the game
- Theme persistence via browser local storage across gaming sessions and device restarts
- Instant theme switching without restarting gameplay or resetting scores
- Accessibility-focused theme options with high contrast variants for WCAG AA compliance
- One-click theme export/import for sharing favorite palettes with friends
- Automatic local storage backup of current session score and all-time best score
- Visual indicators confirming high score data is safely stored locally
- Score data persists across browser sessions, tabs, and page refreshes
- Clear player education on local storage data retention and clearing implications

## Customer Benefits
- Express personal style while enjoying their favorite retro game with complete visual control
- Choose themes optimized for their work environment—dark themes for night gaming, bright themes for daytime play
- Access high-contrast themes designed for players with visual accessibility needs
- Discover and share custom themes with the gaming community
- Know their high scores are permanently tracked and retrievable across all future gaming sessions
- Build competitive motivation through visible, persistent all-time best score records
- Enjoy uninterrupted score tracking even after browser updates or system restarts
- Make Snake Game feel like a truly personalized application rather than a generic web game
